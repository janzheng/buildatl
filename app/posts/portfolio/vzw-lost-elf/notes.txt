The Lost Elf interactive story is a free gift from Verizon, designed to get kids of all ages in the holiday spirit. One of Santa’s helpers has lost his way and he needs a holiday hero to get him home pronto! Design a character and embark on a wacky, wintery adventure as you and the elf chase after the jolly man in the red suit. While on your journey, uncover surprises by clicking or touching items on each page. When you’re finished, save your story and send it to loved ones as a gift.
